Our history.

There is one Rubyville School in Abuja. The Rubyville Primary Schools was founded on the 14 of feburary 2011 in order to provide a British style education of high quality to Nigerian children and to the children of expatriates living and working in the growing city of Abuja, now the capital of Nigeria.
